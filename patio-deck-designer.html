📦 Import a board from a photo

Upload a photo of the product label / spec sheet and (optionally) the “what’s included” panel. Claude reads the board width, length, pieces per pack, pack price and coverage, then adds it as a selectable board. Runs against the Anthropic API with your own key (stored only in this browser).

⚖ Wood vs composite

Same footprint & framing, decking swapped. Composite costs more up front but needs no staining; wood needs re-sealing every ~2 years.
